Display advertising component of Boeing's Epic recruitment initiative, split across two audience tracks — undergraduate and graduate candidates, and established professionals — with nine sub-campaigns targeted by field of expertise: Engineering, Aerospace, Communications, IT, Manufacturing, and Defense. Each sub-campaign produced a four-size banner suite adapted to both tracks, bringing the total to approximately 136 assets. Originally animated; shown here as static composites.
